Last year in all five NYC boroughs, from August 30 to December 24, there were over 900 motor accidents.

New York City is a nightmare when it comes to traffic issues. Crashes happen all the time so I wanted to take a deeper dive into the analystics behind the problem.

I discovered that out of the 986 crashes that took place during the time frame, there were suprisingly only 4 deaths. It is amazing to see the technological advancements that car manufacturers have made over the years.
